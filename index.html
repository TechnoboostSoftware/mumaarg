FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Maarg?

Maarg is a platform offering 1:1 mentorship for choosing the right college, deciding on a course, and navigating college applications. Our mentors are individuals who have successfully been through these experiences.

Who are the mentors on Maarg

Maarg connects you with a network of 1000+ university students, fresh graduates, and established professionals from diverse fields. They all have experience navigating the post-12th path and are passionate about guiding students like you.

How can mentors help in choosing the right college?

Maarg mentors have successfully cracked entrance exams, explored various institutions, and can provide valuable insights on different colleges and courses.

What kind of help can I get from a mentor?

Your mentor can help you with anything related to your college decisions, such as Choosing the right college or course based on your interests and aptitude. Preparing for entrance exams and navigating the application process. Giving you honest feedback about their colleges and course What entrance exams to give

How do I find the right mentor for me?

Browse through mentors who have been where you want to go. Choose someone whose story resonates with yours.

Is scheduling a session easy on Maarg?

Absolutely! Maarg offers hassle-free scheduling. Choose a time that suits you with simple planning.

How does Maarg ensure the quality of mentorship?

Maarg values quality mentorship. We review mentor profiles, and feedback from mentees is considered to maintain the highest standards.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to have prior mentoring experience?

No prior mentoring experience is required. Your unique experiences and insights are what make you a valuable mentor.

What kind of commitment is expected from mentors?

The commitment is flexible and based on your availability. Whether you can spare a few hours a week or more, you can contribute meaningfully.

How does the onboarding process work?

After your application, we'll schedule a 30-minute call to verify your profile and onboard you seamlessly into the Maarg community.

Is there any cost involved in becoming a mentor

No, there is no cost to become a mentor on Maarg. It's a platform for sharing knowledge, and we want to make mentorship accessible to all.
